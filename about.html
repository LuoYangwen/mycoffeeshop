About our coffee

Will Rock Your Socks Off

Because I want to present the good, so I keep trying.

The owner opened the coffee shop to promote the concept of protecting the environment, caring for the earth, and being animal-friendly, and in addition to plastic reduction, the products used in the store give priority to products and ingredients that can sustain the environment and seek animal welfare. For example, we use Rainforest Alliance certified coffee that does not cut down the rainforest and maintains biodiversity; we use raw materials and recipes that are not tested on animals; we choose eggs that are not caged and raised without drugs as ingredients for desserts...etc. Through these specific practices, we have gained consumer recognition that we can love the earth while drinking coffee.

If you have time or just passing by, come and rest your feet.

We provide our customers with the best coffee possible. We have a standardized process to produce great tasting coffee of consistent quality and taste, providing our customers with safe and secure coffee. Experience the warmth of holding a cup in your hands.
